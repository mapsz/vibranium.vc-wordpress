Become Stronger with a Trusted and Founder-Friendly VC Partner

Building a fast-growing international company is somewhat like climbing a mountain. You need tools and equipment, a clear vision of your goal, and enough stamina to reach it. At Vibranium, we know what it takes to reach the top because we come from an entrepreneurial background ourselves. A close-knit team of six, we formed a VC fund 1 year ago in Silicon Valley, the ultimate startup hub that became our home.

When working with startups, we rely on a combination of experience, knowledge, and instinct, similar to how an alpinist relies on a mix of physical skill, mental preparation, and intuition. Through careful evaluation, we choose companies with great potential to win global markets — and help them navigate this difficult but exciting journey.

Why Vibranium?

Building a startup can be tough and full of unexpected challenges. It takes a lot of resilience to counter difficulties and uncertainty on a daily basis. This is when you need a trusted VC partner whom you can rely on to stand next to you on this journey.

That's the kind of partner that we want to be. We fundamentally believe that a great VC investor provides much more than financial backing. That means profound expertise in startup acceleration and business scaling, access to a wide network, and unlimited support when navigating risky bends. On top of that, we have launched a Softlanding Program to help promising startups relocate to the US and feel at home in the Valley prior to starting their global journey.

We are all about the startup team. This means we will take an effort to deeply understand your goals and needs and provide support exactly when you need it. We will also warn you about pitfalls — including those in your own processes or products. Don’t let this discourage you. Recognizing your weaknesses is the initial step towards building your strength.

Our Philosophy

We support innovative startups for whom their impact is as important as financial gains. This is why we supported Otis, an AI-based platform that helps small businesses grow and engage customers by leveling the ads competition field for them. Or iSchoolConnect, another portfolio company of ours, which makes college admissions more accessible to students from all over the world.

We care about the ethics behind each one of our projects. This means there are certain legal markets that we don't and won't invest in: alcohol and tobacco, legalized drugs, weapons, the adult industry, gambling, financial scheming. Because it's not about the money, it's about what we bring into the world.

We endorse the companies that build their business relationships with care rather than take investment from anyone, and we share our knowledge of the VC industry to help make smart decisions.

Kirill Timofeev

Co-Founder, launched his business career at the age of 20 when he organized a professional MMA team.

Our larger mission is to improve VC fundraising landscape. We want startups to better understand the hidden obstacles in the process, so we provide startups who come to us with better understanding, structure, some guidelines, and advice on how to raise seed funding with a higher chance of success.

“We endorse the companies that build their business relationships with care rather than take investment from anyone, and we share our knowledge of the VC industry to help make smart decisions,” says Kirill Timofeev, Vibranium Co-Founder.

We have a strong passion about what we do — and we hope so are you. Because no matter how great due diligence results might be, no success is possible without the inner drive. Let's explore this drive and win together.

Choose your VC partner wisely. Choose Vibranium.
